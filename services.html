SERVICES


Great dental health is the result of early detection and preventive treatments. But it’s never too late to develop a connection with your dental team and get the care you need!

INVISALIGN

Lots of people have crooked teeth — about 300 million! Thankfully, there’s an easy solution. At Perfect smile Dental, we offer Invisalign as our preferred tooth-straightening system for people of all ages. Invisalign is a great option for those wanting to avoid the hassle, discomfort, and time commitment of traditional metal braces. This highly effective treatment uses a series of clear, custom-made aligners and offers beautiful results.

VENEERS

If you have cosmetic concerns about your teeth, porcelain veneers are one of the quickest, most dramatic ways to improve your smile. We like to refer to veneers as “instant orthodontics” — the faster, simpler way to align and perfect your teeth without the need for braces. Veneers are thin, durable, and custom-made porcelain shells for your teeth.

CROWN

Our porcelain crowns are custom designed to fit your mouth. We specialize in tooth-colored crowns that look just like your natural teeth. Plus, thanks to our advanced technology, we offer same day crown options to fit your busy life.

DENTURES

If you’re missing multiple teeth due to decay, injury, or the natural aging process, dentures might be the perfect solution. Modern dentures are made from high-tech, durable materials and are much easier to maintain than you might think. When properly designed and fitted, your dentures will act just like natural teeth, restoring your ability to smile, speak, and chew with confidence.

IMPLANTS

When you’re dealing with missing, broken, or severely damaged teeth, your confidence can suffer. More importantly, these issues take a toll on your health. You deserve better. Talk to us about dental implant restorations. At Perfect smile Dental, our implant restorations are permanent and made from the highest-quality biocompatible materials.
